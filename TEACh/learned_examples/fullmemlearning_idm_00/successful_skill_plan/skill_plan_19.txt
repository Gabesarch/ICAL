1. Go to the knife next to the sink and pick it up.
2. Go to the bread on the dining table and slice it.
3. Initialize a new sliced bread object.
4. Go to the toaster next to the coffee maker and place a slice of bread in it.
5. Toggle the toaster on, wait for the bread to toast, then toggle the toaster off.
6. Remove the toasted bread slice and place it on the dining table.
7. Repeat steps 4-6 for another slice of bread.
8. Go to the lettuce next to the bread and slice it.
9. Initialize a new sliced lettuce object.
10. Place the toasted bread slices and sliced lettuce on a plate to assemble the sandwich components.
