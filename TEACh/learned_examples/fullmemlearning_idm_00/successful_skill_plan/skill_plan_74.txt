1. Check if the faucet is on and turn it off to conserve water.
2. Define InteractionObject instances for the mugs that need to be cleaned.
3. For each dirty mug:
   a. Go to the mug and pick it up.
   b. If the mug is filled with liquid, pour it out into the sink basin.
   c. Place the mug in the sink basin.
   d. Turn on the faucet to rinse the mug with water.
   e. Update the mug's state to reflect that it is no longer dirty.
   f. Turn off the faucet after cleaning.
   g. Pick up the clean mug and place it back on the countertop.
