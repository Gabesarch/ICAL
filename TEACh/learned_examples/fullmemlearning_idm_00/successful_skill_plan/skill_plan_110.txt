1. Check if the faucet is on; if so, go to the faucet and turn it off.
2. Go to the mug and pick it up.
3. If the mug is filled, pour out the contents into the sink basin and update the state to not filled and "fillLiquid" to None.
4. If the mug is dirty, place it in the sink basin, turn on the faucet to rinse it, update the mug's state to not dirty, and then turn off the faucet.
5. Place the clean mug back in the sink basin.
