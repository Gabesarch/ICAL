1. Go to the dirty mug and pick it up.
2. Go to the sink basin.
3. If the faucet is on, turn it off.
4. Pour out the contents of the mug into the sink basin.
5. Place the mug under the faucet.
6. Turn on the faucet to rinse the mug.
7. Turn off the faucet after rinsing.
8. Update the mug's state to reflect that it is no longer dirty.
9. Place the clean mug on the countertop.
