1. Go to the knife and pick it up.
2. Go to the bread and slice it.
3. Initialize a new InteractionObject for the sliced bread.
4. Go to the toaster and place the bread slice in it.
5. Toggle the toaster on to start toasting.
6. Toggle the toaster off after toasting is done and update the bread slice's "cooked" state to True.
7. Pick up the toasted bread slice.
8. Go to the clean plate and place the toasted bread slice on it.
