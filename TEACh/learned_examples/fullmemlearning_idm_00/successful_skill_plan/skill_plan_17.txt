1. Go to the knife and pick it up.

3. Initialize a new sliced bread object.
4. Go to the toaster and place a slice of bread in it.
5. Toggle the toaster on and wait for the bread to toast.
6. Toggle the toaster off and remove the toasted bread slice.
7. Check if the plate is clean; if not, clean it.
8. Place the toasted bread slice on the clean plate under the salt shaker on the dining table.
