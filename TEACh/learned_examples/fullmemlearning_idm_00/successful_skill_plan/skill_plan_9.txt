1. Go to the knife and pick it up.
2. Go to the bread and slice it.
3. Initialize two slices of bread as new InteractionObjects.
4. Go to the toaster and place the first slice of bread in it.
5. Wait for the bread to toast and then update its state to toasted.
6. Pick up the toasted bread and put it down on the countertop.
7. Place the second slice of bread in the toaster.
8. Wait for the second slice to toast and then update its state to toasted.
9. Pick up the toasted bread and put it down on the countertop.
10. Go to the tomato, pick it up, and slice it.
11. Initialize a slice of tomato as a new InteractionObject.
12. Check if the plate is clean, if not, clean it at the sink.
13. Assemble the sandwich on the clean plate with the toasted bread and tomato slice.
